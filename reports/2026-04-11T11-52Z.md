# TON Monitor Report — 2026-04-11 11:52 UTC

## Summary
The dominant story this hour is TON's Catchain 2.0 mainnet upgrade (live ~April 10), which cuts block times from 2.5 s to 400 ms and delivers ~1-second transaction finality — Pavel Durov calls it "step 1 of 7" of his MTONGA roadmap. Despite the landmark technical milestone, TON's price remains under pressure (~$1.21–$1.28), with no significant capital rotation into the asset. On the ecosystem side, Rakuten Wallet is listing TON for spot trading starting April 15 in Japan, and Wallet in Telegram's perpetual-futures integration (via Lighter DEX) continues to expand the platform's DeFi reach.
