- Block time: 2.5s → ~400ms; confirmation time: ~1 second
- Announced by Pavel Durov as Step 1 of 7 in the **Make TON Great Again (MTONGA)** initiative
- Next planned step: 6× reduction in transaction fees
- Binance supported the upgrade with scheduled maintenance on April 7
