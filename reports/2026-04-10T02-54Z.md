## Active Promotions

### TON Foundation / Ecosystem

- **Catchain 2.0 Launch (MTONGA Step 1) — April 7–10, 2026**
  - Pavel Durov announced the official activation of sub-second finality on TON mainnet.
  - Block times: 2.5s → 400 ms. Transaction confirmations: ~1 second.
  - Quote (Durov, Telegram channel): *"The network has sped up by approximately 10 times, block production has increased by 6 times, and transactions are now almost instantaneous."*
  - This is **Step 1 of 7** in the **MTONGA ("Make TON Great Again")** roadmap.
  - **Next step (Step 2):** Cut already-low transaction fees by 6×.
  - Source: [ton.org — TON Is Now Up to 6x Faster](https://ton.org/en/Ton_is_now_up_to_6x_faster)

- **TON Pay — New Payments Layer**
  - Official TON Pay initiative announced: a new payments infrastructure layer for the TON ecosystem.
  - Broader merchant rollout and new payment use cases planned throughout 2026.
  - Gateway Conference (May 2026) expected to bring further product updates.
  - Source: [ton.org — TON Pay](https://ton.org/en/ton-pay-a-new-payments-layer)

### Wallet in Telegram
