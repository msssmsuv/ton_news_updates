## Active Promotions

### Catchain 2.0 Mainnet Upgrade (April 7–10, 2026)
- **What:** TON's most significant consensus upgrade to date — sub-second transaction finality (400ms blocks, ~1s confirmation).
- **Announcement:** Pavel Durov stated the network sped up ~10×, with block production up 6×.
- **Exchange support:** Binance paused TON deposits/withdrawals on April 7 for maintenance; Bitget and other exchanges similarly notified users.
- **Validator timeline:** Nodes upgraded by March 31 → basechain vote April 2 → full masterchain activation April 7.
- Source: [coingabbar.com](https://www.coingabbar.com/en/crypto-currency-news/ton-mainnet-upgrade-three-phase-full-activation-april-7-2026)
