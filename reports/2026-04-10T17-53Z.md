# TON Monitor Report — 2026-04-10 17:53 UTC

## Summary

The dominant story this hour is the live activation of TON's Catchain 2.0 mainnet upgrade on April 10, 2026, delivering sub-second finality (~1 s confirmations, 400 ms block times) and a 6× increase in block production speed — the most significant protocol change in the network's history. Pavel Durov confirmed the launch and called it "step 1 of 7 to Make TON Great Again (MTONGA)," with fee cuts of 6× announced as the next milestone. Despite the technical milestone, TON's price remained under pressure, trading near $1.21–$1.28, as markets had already priced in the upgrade.
