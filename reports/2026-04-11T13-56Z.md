# TON Monitor Report — 2026-04-11 13:56 UTC

## Summary
The dominant story this hour is the TON Catchain 2.0 mainnet upgrade (live since ~April 7–10), which reduced block times from 2.5 s to 400 ms and delivers sub-second transaction finality — the biggest protocol change in the network's history and Step 1 of the "Make TON Great Again" roadmap. Paradoxically, Toncoin's price slid ~4% to ~$1.21–1.28 despite the upgrade, though daily trading volume hit $144.49 M on April 10. On the exchange side, Rakuten Wallet announced Toncoin spot trading starting April 15, 2026, and Wallet in Telegram launched perpetual futures via Lighter DEX integration on April 2.
