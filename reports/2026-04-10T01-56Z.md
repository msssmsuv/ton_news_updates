### Catchain 2.0 / MTONGA Roadmap (April 10, 2026)
- TON mainnet now processes blocks every ~400ms (down from 2.5s); full transactions confirm in ~1 second
- Pavel Durov announced "Make TON Great Again" (MTONGA) — a 7-step plan; this is Step 1
- Step 2: targeting a 6x reduction in transaction fees (timeline TBD)
- Binance scheduled coordinated exchange maintenance on April 7 to support the upgrade
- **Official announcement:** [ton.org](https://ton.org/en/Ton_is_now_up_to_6x_faster)

- TON ecosystem now supports 50+ million wallets
- **Source:** [BeInCrypto](https://beincrypto.com/ton-scrypt-usdt-infrastructure/) · [Crypto Reporter](https://www.crypto-reporter.com/press-releases/ton-partners-with-scrypt-to-enable-institutional-access-to-stablecoins-124476/)
